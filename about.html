About me

Musician & Front-end developer

I grew up in Syria, lived and studied there until 2015 when I moved to the UK to do an MA in Music Education at UCL. I have been a freelance musician for a long time and I love it. I write music, play live concerts, and I teach music to children. I also lead community music workshops.

In the last few months, I started feeling drawn to coding as a career that can be suitable for my wider interests, and I thought that learning how to code might give me more freedom to work from anywhere in the world.

For my coding aspirations, I'd love to help grassroots organizations and humanitarian initiatives build attractive platforms as well as building interactive music-education websites for children to enjoy and for teachers to organize their work.
